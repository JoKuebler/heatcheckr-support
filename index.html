How it works

Built on real play-by-play data.

Every game is analyzed using 20+ factors from official NBA data. No guessing ‚Äî just smart scores and clear labels.

01

Games are analyzed

Every morning, overnight NBA play-by-play data is processed through a custom scoring engine that weighs 20+ factors like lead changes, clutch plays, and game flow.

02

Each game receives an excitement score from 1‚Äì10. A 9 is a thriller. A 2 is a snoozer. No ambiguity ‚Äî just a number that tells you what you need to know.

03

Smart labels tell you exactly what made a game exciting (or boring). "Photo Finish" and "Chaos Ball" hit different ‚Äî and now you know before you press play.
