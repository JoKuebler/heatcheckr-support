Frequently Asked Questions

How are excitement scores calculated?
I analyze every game using 20+ factors including final margin, lead changes, clutch plays, overtime, game flow, pace, and scoring patterns. Each factor is weighted based on its impact on game excitement, then combined into a 0-10 score. Higher scores mean more exciting games!
When is data updated?
Data is updated every morning around 6 AM CET with analysis of yesterday's games. Scheduled games for today are also shown so you can see what's coming up.
Can I see games from previous days?
Yes! You can swipe left to browse historical games from past days. This is great for catching up on games you might have missed throughout the week.

Can I filter which labels I see?
Yes! Tap the settings icon (three lines) in the top right corner to toggle different label categories on or off. This lets you customize what information you see for each game.
Is this app affiliated with the NBA?
No, HeatCheckr is an independent app created by a fan. I use publicly available data from NBA.com and link to official NBA highlight videos. I'm not endorsed by or affiliated with the NBA.
Why is the app free?
I built this as a passion project to solve my own problem as a European NBA fan. There's an optional "Buy me a Coffee" support option to help cover server costs, but the core app is and will remain free.
Do you plan to add more features?
Absolutely! I have many ideas for future updates including more detailed game breakdowns, playoff tracking, and personalization options. If you have feature requests, feel free to email me!

What Data We Collect

HeatCheckr is designed to be privacy-friendly. Here's what we do and don't collect:

  • No personal information: We don't ask for your name, email, or any identifying information.
  • No account required: You can use HeatCheckr without creating an account.
  • No analytics tracking: We don't use third-party analytics services to track your behavior.
  • No advertising: We don't display ads or share data with advertisers.

Local Storage

HeatCheckr stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Cached game data: Recent game information is cached to improve app performance and allow offline viewing.
  • Your preferences: Settings like which label categories you've toggled are stored locally on your device.
  • Warning acknowledgments: We remember if you've seen certain one-time warnings (like the highlight spoiler warning).

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Network Requests

The app makes network requests to:

  • Our API server: To fetch NBA game data and excitement scores. These requests don't include any personal information.
  • NBA.com: When you tap to view highlights, you're taken to the official NBA website. We don't control their privacy practices.

Third-Party Services

HeatCheckr uses the following third-party services:

  • NBA API: We fetch publicly available game statistics from the NBA. No user data is shared.
  • Railway: Our backend is hosted on Railway. Server logs may contain standard HTTP request information (IP addresses, request timestamps) but we don't actively collect or analyze this data.

Children's Privacy

HeatCheckr does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages as it simply displays sports statistics.
