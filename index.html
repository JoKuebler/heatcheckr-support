Features

More than just scores.

HeatCheckr keeps evolving. Here's what's already built in — and what's coming next.

Favorite Team Alerts

Pick your team and get a push notification once a day when they played an exciting game. Never miss a great performance from your squad.

Star Player Warning

See at a glance when a team's star players sat out a game. HeatCheckr flags missing stars right on the game card so you always have the full context behind the score.

One-Tap Highlights

Every game card has a highlight button that takes you straight to the spoiler free recaps. Choose between YouTube or NBA.com as your preferred source of highlights.

Heat Report

The week's Top 5 most exciting games, ranked and spoiler-free. Add your favorite teams to see every game they played ranked by excitement, switch between Weekly and Monthly views, and browse the whole season back to opening night. Perfect for finding the best matchups to watch over the weekend.

Daily Briefing

Start your day with a spoiler-free digest of the NBA: the biggest storyline, a game worth watching, and news from around the league — never a score or a winner. The Main Story is free for everyone; Heat Pass unlocks the full briefing. Perfect for staying in the loop through the off-season too.

How it works

Built on real NBA & WNBA play-by-play data.

Every game is analyzed using 20+ factors from official NBA & WNBA data. Smart excitement scores and clear labels.

01

Games are analyzed

Every morning, overnight NBA & WNBA play-by-play data is processed through a custom scoring engine that weighs 20+ factors like lead changes, clutch plays, and game flow.

02

You get a score

Each game receives an excitement score from 1–10. A 9 is a thriller. A 2 is a snoozer. No ambiguity — just a number that tells you what you need to know.

03

Labels explain why

Smart labels tell you exactly what made a game exciting (or boring). "Photo Finish" and "Chaos Ball" hit different — and now you know before you press play.
